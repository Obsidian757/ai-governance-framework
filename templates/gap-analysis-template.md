## How to use this template

This is a reusable per-engagement worksheet. For each clause and Annex A control, set:

- **Status:** ✅ Met · ⚠️ Partial · ❌ Not met · N/A (with justification)
- **Evidence:** path/URL to artifact, interview reference, or screenshot
- **Gap:** specific deficiency in one sentence
- **Risk:** L/M/H (likelihood × impact)
- **Recommended action:** quick win / project / strategic
- **Effort:** S (≤ 1 wk) / M (1–4 wk) / L (>1 mo)
- **Owner:** role responsible for closure
- **Target date:** YYYY-MM-DD

Delete this instruction block before delivery.
